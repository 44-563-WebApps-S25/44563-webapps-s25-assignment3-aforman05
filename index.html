Instructions for Vacuuming Stairs

  1. Locate the vacuum
  2. Bring vacuum towards the stairs
  3. Unwrap the cord
  4. Locate the closest outlet to the stairs and plug in the vacuum there
  5. Vacuum the first set of stairs
    • Remove the attachment for the vacuum that is most flat
    • Insert the attachment into the vacuum, you may have to remove a stick looking piece for the attachment
    • Turn the vacuum on
    • Go step by step moving the vacuum attachment along the vertical plane followed by the horizontal plane of the stair
    • Turn off the vacuum when you reach the landing
    • Remove the attachment and put the stick piece back
    • Empty vacuum if full
  6. Vacuum the landing between stair sets
    • Turn on vacuum
    • Push the vacuum by handle running the bottom over the entire area of the landing
    • Turn off the vacuum
  7. Repeat step 4 and 5 for all remaining sets of steps and landings unplugging and replugging the vacuum if needed
  8. Unplug the vacuum
  9. Wrap up the cord
  10. Put the vacuum back where it was found
